The Five Types

  • The Five Types Of Happiness is a general theory of happiness and motivation.


    It connects work on happiness from people such as Seligman (Positive psychology) and Maslow (Hierarchy of needs), as well as more spiritual stuff such Eckhart Tolle's work on the Ego and Gurdjieff's theory of spiritual development.

    You can use it to explain what makes you happy, but the larger theory (with the 5 happiness enneagram) seems to be compatible with a lot of other theories of personality, motivation and personal growth, so it can be used to tie everything together.


    The basic theory is that there are five types of happiness; Body, Mind, Heart, Spirit and God, and the more satisfied each type is, the happier you are.



    For more details on the theory, you can read some articles.
