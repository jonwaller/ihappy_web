Mind Questions
Introduction

  • After increasing your body happiness, mind happiness becomes important. This is the feeling of "I'm enough", where you work to add to your life, not to avoid negatives. You can set goals on "want" not "need", and it's easier to connect with people since you can let go a little.

Self-love

  • You need to boost your opinion of yourself. Do you feel proud of yourself? When people interact with you, are their lives made better or worse? Concentrate on building your skills and situation so you are happy with yourself.

Codependency

  • If you live your life feeling you lack something, you'll find it hard to take rejection, and will work very hard to keep everything stable and safe. You often carry this feeling from childhood experiences. If you can't forgive yourself, in adulthood you will be prone to addiction and will find it difficult to let go.
