Spirit Questions
Introduction

  • Spirit is about your place in your community. It is about your contribution.

    You are a member of many communities. Your family, work and local groups are all examples of your communities. Online groups count too. When you give generously to these groups, putting aside your short-term happiness for the success of the group, spirit happiness comes out.

Selfless-love

  • The important part of Spirit is giving to your group, when the act is not for your direct benefit. Your personal happiness is important, but it sometimes putting up with a little hardship for the sake of the group's success is a source of happiness. This is Spirit.

Care

  • Unloving contribution doesn't make you happy. When you care about what you do, and the group you are in, and are working to help that group, you experience joy.

Quality

  • Again, quality is more important over quantity. Assuming you love all the groups equally, working hard for a small number of groups brings more joy than spreading your attention over a larger number of groups.
