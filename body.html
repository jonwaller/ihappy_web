Body Questions
Introduction

  • For your total happiness, your body happiness is fundamental. Before working on higher happiness categories like relationships and spirituality, you need boost your body happiness.

Sleep

  • Sleep is very important to happiness. Make sure you get at least 7 hours, and don't sleep over 9 unless you're catching up.

Diet

  • Eat natural. Study after study points to the "paleo" diet, eating the kind of materials we evolved to eat, as being the best diet for long term health, energy and resistance to disease.

Exercise

  • Find something you enjoy, and can do daily. Jog to your station and back, try a fitness app or take up swimming. Try it for a week at be amazed how your daily happiness improves.

Sensations

  • Sensations are important too. This includes having a coat for a cold day, feeling clean, sexual sensations and non-sexual touching (hugs, for example).
